Bringing the Fun of D&D to You!

Dungeons & Dragons is a roleplaying game where each player takes on the role of a hero — a valiant fighter, a spell-slinging wizard, or anything in between. Players set out on adventures, defeat vicious monsters, and earn glory. D&D builds problem-solving skills, sparks imagination, and teaches teamwork — all while having a blast.

You play in the comfort of your own home. I bring everything needed — dice, pencils, maps, miniatures, ambient music. Choose from a wide selection of adventures or have a custom adventure written just for you.

Click the sections below to learn more.

One-Shot Dungeon Crawls: Dank crypts, treacherous marshes, drow-haunted Underdark, orc war parties, evil necromancers. Perfect for a single themed session — no long-term commitment. Ideal for bachelor parties, corporate team-building, or shaking up game night.

Campaign Long-Form Campaigns: A series of continuing adventures woven into an epic branching story. For groups who want to meet regularly and play through legendary storylines. View the campaign selection.

Party Children's Parties: A fantastic adventure for your kids and their friends. Light on dark themes, heavy on imagination, math, puzzle-solving, and teamwork. Recommended for ages 10+.

Online Virtual Sessions: Can't host in person? I also run fully online sessions via video call with digital maps and animated battle boards. Same experience, anywhere.

Contact me for available dates, then we discuss the type of adventure or campaign. Dungeon crawl one-shots run in 2-hour or 4-hour blocks. Campaign sessions range from 4 to 8 hours.

After we agree, I do the rest. Players can create their own characters or I'll provide pre-made characters of appropriate level in the classes of their choosing. I show up at your door with everything: characters, miniatures, battle maps, dice, ambient music.

Premium Upgrade — 40" TV with Animated Battle Maps:
Fully immersive digital tabletop with live tactical maps, dynamic lighting, and cinematic effects. Available as an add-on for in-person sessions.

Wizards of the Coast released the updated 2024 Player's Handbook — a significant revision to 5th edition with streamlined rules, new subclasses, and rebalanced gameplay. Think of it as 5e perfected, not replaced.

I run both the classic 2014 rules and the new 2024 revision. New players can jump straight into the updated edition; veterans can stick with what they know. Either way, the adventure is the same — just how we roll changes.
