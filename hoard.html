Words of Power

The sealed chests answer only to those who uncover the realm's secrets — eggs and runes hidden across these pages. Here is what the old seekers knew:

  • Look where the gold flickers. Faint sigils hide in the realm's words; they glow when your cursor passes over them. Collect a full set, and a chest opens.
  • A true mage casts spells through the keys beneath their fingers. Simply type a word of power — a name, a legend, a cursed place — and the realm will answer. The bold know many such words.
  • Roll the bones, wander to the end, and visit when the veil is thin. Fortune favors the curious.
